Located at the bustling heart of the city, Church Street has always served as the heart of Bangalore’s culture scene. As new forms of creation declared guerrilla warfare on the city, it quickly gained fame as the place where urban culture, street graffiti and artistic mayhem meet! Of course, the first ever Social outpost at Church Street fit right in and quickly became an anchor for the nightlife and culture scene in the city. Four years later, on June 14th 2018, the outlet unveiled its brand new iteration - the #ARTlet, aka a bar, cafe and co-working space where you’re free to #ExpressYOUrself! Church Street Social 2.0 is a shape-shifting gallery full of radical art put together by some of the finest creatives in the country. It's a studio, a showcase, and a space that encourages experimental minds to come together and collaborate. From sound-reactive neo-pixel installations to a chewing gum hammock, a giant dervish projection and a dot chair that beguiles the mind - there are so many elements of Church Street Social that need to be witnessed in person! Displayed artworks are on sale, so that you can take a piece of the creativity home with you, or grab some of the paints and brushes lying around and fashion your own masterpiece right there! With new forms of art at every turn, and tools galore, #ChurchStreetSocial hopes to light the spark of creativity in creators of all kinds.
